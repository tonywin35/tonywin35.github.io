Common questions

How do I report a note or a person?

Tap the ••• menu on any note in a friend’s feed, or the ••• menu on a profile, and choose Report. Pick a reason — we review every report.

How do I block someone?

From a profile, tap ••• → Block, or use the ••• menu on one of their notes. Blocking hides their notes from you and yours from them, and removes any follow between you. Manage blocks in Settings → Blocked accounts.

How do I delete my account?

Settings → Delete account, then confirm with your password. This permanently removes your profile, notes, and follow relationships and cannot be undone.

Who can see my notes?

Notes are private by default. A note is visible to others only when you mark it public, and only according to your account’s privacy setting.

Report objectionable content or abuse

If you’ve encountered content or behavior that violates our Terms, report it in-app or email tonywins35@gmail.com with details. We remove violating content and offending accounts.
